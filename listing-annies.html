About Annie's

On many internets polls Annie’s was voted Thailand’s Best. Now we have reopened our with modern new rooms 2 different styles we are ready to make our mark again. Annie’s due to its past popularity has a long hard road to get it past position back and besides by doing what we did before we have one more special thing to announce, the original Annie herself will be running our new site.
